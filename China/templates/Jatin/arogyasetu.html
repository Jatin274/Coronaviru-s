About Arogya setu app :

By Govt. of India

In 2020, During this covid-19 pandemic , the government of India pulished an app known ass Aarogya setu app in public interest, it is widely accepted by the indian users around more than 2 Million people are using it. It uses bluetooth and gps technology to detect the infected persons nearby whose data is with the govt. servers, it highly recommended to install it now (if not done yet), and ensure safety of yourself and your family.

The key features of Aarogya setu app:-

They are as :-

  • It detects the diagnosed/ positive persons nearby
  • It keeps you updated with the latest news
  • It spreads awareness about the corona virus infection
  • It accomodates several government facilities
  • You can opt for urgent medical facilities using it.
